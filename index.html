How Kaizen Flow Works

From document to compliant output — a streamlined, auditable path.

  1. Upload your CI/PL/B/L/AWB (native or scanned PDF).
  2. AI extraction identifies key fields (party info, incoterms, commodity, values, packages).
  3. Mapping to form schemas (K1–K9, Borang 2) and EDIFACT/CUSCAR segments per MIG.
  4. Validation runs rule checks (codes, date/qty/value math, required fields) and flags issues.
  5. Preview & Edit — operators confirm/adjust before finalizing.
  6. Export to Excel (forms) or EDIFACT/CUSCAR messages; logs stored for audit.
  7. (M2B) Send via Dagang Net and receive CUSRES/EMSRSP responses.
